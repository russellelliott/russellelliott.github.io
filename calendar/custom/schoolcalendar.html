Year Round School Plans

There are seveal variations of a year-round school year calendar. Each of these systems has 180 days of schooling. All these options have a 4 week summer break after their scheduled break.

  • 45-15 (45 days of school, followed by 15 days of break)
  • 60-20 (60 days of school, followed by 20 days of break)
  • 90-30 (90 days of school, followed by 30 days of break)
